At a high level, Global Gold turns a verified bar of allocated gold into a
standardized, liquid, on-chain asset without ever breaking the chain of title.

## The lifecycle

1. **Register** — a bar's title is recorded in the **Title Register**, the
   authoritative record of who owns what metal, where.
2. **Tokenize** — verified title is represented on-chain through the
   **Tokenization** module.
3. **Collateralize** — tokens can be posted as collateral under the
   **Collateralization** module.
4. **Make liquid** — the **Liquidity** module connects holders to markets.
5. **Reserve & secure** — the **Reserve** and **Security** modules underwrite
   solvency and protect the system end to end.

## Why a shared standard

Without a common standard, every vault, refiner, and venue speaks its own
dialect. Global Gold defines one — operated by the operating company under the
governance described later in these docs — so allocated gold can settle the
same way everywhere.
