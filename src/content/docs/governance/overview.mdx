Global Gold is a shared standard, and a shared standard needs stewardship. This
section describes how the protocol is governed — who sets parameters, how
changes are made, and how the operating company is held accountable.

## Principles

- The standard is operated transparently and in the interest of all participants.
- Risk and reserve parameters are set through governance, not unilaterally.
- The operating company runs the standard within those governance constraints.
