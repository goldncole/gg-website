The **Liquidity** module connects holders of tokenized gold to markets, so
allocated metal can be bought, sold, and settled globally under one standard.

## What it enables

Liquidity turns standardized tokens into tradable instruments across on-chain
and off-chain venues, while title and reserves remain anchored in the protocol.

## Settlement

Trades settle against tokenized title, so liquidity never decouples from the
underlying allocated gold.
