The **Reserve** module underwrites the solvency of the system — the assurance
that tokenized claims are fully backed by allocated metal.

## Backing

Every unit in circulation is matched to real gold recorded in the Title Register.
The Reserve module makes that backing measurable and verifiable.

## Transparency

Reserve state is designed to be auditable, so holders and counterparties can
confirm that the standard remains fully backed.
