The **Title Register** is the foundation of the protocol: the authoritative
record of gold title across the network. Every downstream module — Tokenization,
Collateralization, Liquidity, Reserve, and Security — depends on it.

## What it records

The register tracks specific, allocated bars and the chain of title attached to
each: which holder owns the metal, which vault custodies it, and the events that
have moved or encumbered it.

## Why it comes first

Nothing is tokenized or collateralized until its title is recorded here. The
register is the single point of truth the rest of the standard builds on.
