The **Tokenization** module represents verified title from the Title Register as
on-chain tokens, without breaking the chain of custody or ownership.

## From title to token

Once a bar's title is recorded, Tokenization issues a standardized on-chain
representation of it. The token is a faithful proxy for the underlying metal —
redeemable, transferable, and auditable.

## Standardization

Tokenization is where allocated gold becomes fungible at the protocol level
through Standard Metal Units, so it can move across venues under one standard.
