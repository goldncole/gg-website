**Conditional Claim Tokens (CCT)** represent a claim on gold that resolves once
defined conditions are satisfied — for example, verification or settlement steps
in the tokenization flow.

## Lifecycle

A CCT is issued against pending title and converts to a settled representation
once its conditions clear, at which point it can become a Standard Metal Unit.
