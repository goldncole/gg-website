**gBars** are an on-chain representation of specific allocated bars — closest to
the physical metal in the token family, preserving the identity of individual
bars recorded in the Title Register.

## Use

gBars suit holders who want a direct link to particular bars rather than a fully
pooled, fungible exposure.
