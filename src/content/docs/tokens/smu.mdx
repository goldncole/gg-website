**Standard Metal Units (SMU)** are the protocol's standardized, fungible unit of
allocated gold. They are how verified metal becomes interchangeable across the
network while staying anchored to specific title.

## Why standardize

A single unit lets gold from different vaults, refiners, and regions trade under
one shared standard — the basis for the network's liquidity.
