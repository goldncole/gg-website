**U.S. Gold (USG)** is a jurisdiction-specific Standard Metal Unit, representing
allocated gold custodied under U.S. arrangements.

## Relationship to SMU

USG inherits the SMU standard and adds the jurisdictional context relevant to
holders and venues operating in the United States.
